Retreats

The Retreats are times to come together. They are not times to teach doctrine as it were, rather they are times where we emphasize on our behavioral and attitudinal dispositions from the Word of God.

Retreats are times we can actually come to a place of re-evaluation and re-examining of our lives even as the scripture says in 2Cor13:5 that we should "examine our lives whether we be in the Faith", it is thus very important to take heed unto ourselves, and this quarterly retreat are a time to do this. They also give us an opportunity to do better in the next quarter.
