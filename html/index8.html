✓

A global youth ministry sent to raise a new generation of Christ centered leaders

✓

To provide a forum for fellowship and Spiritual development of youths from high school to undergraduate life in Nigeria and Globally

 ✓

To maintain the Spiritual well-being of its members, within and outside the school environment

 ✓

To provide avenues for evangelism through outreach programs, Bible Studies, Prayer Meetings and other Christian programs

To reach out to the lost, groom and establish them in the faith

 ✓

To take care of peculiar needs and interest of its members, such as those bordering on emotional and academic matters
