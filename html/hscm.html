High School Camp Meetings

Our High School Camp Meeting is an opportunity and a platform to bring together all the High schoolers we have been reaching out to in their individual secondary schools to a place of retreat, a place of fellowship with God in prayers, in worship, in listening to the Word and in co-habitation and camaraderie with one another as young people.

We believe strongly that the way to help the young people is not to give them entertainment, but to give them strength from the Word of God. This will help them to know what to do for their lives and many people have come to a place of acknowledgment, discipline, realization and focus from this camp meetings. This camp meetings date back to nine years ago and we keep going on strong year in, year out.
