Prayer Meeting

Prayer meeting is a beautiful time to fellowship with the Father. We believe that in the place of prayer, we are not just communicating with our Father, but we are making tremendous power available, addressing situations and enforcing the will of God on the earth. Little wonder Jesus said in Luke 18:1that "Men ought always to pray and not to loose heart, faint or give up". This is why, we take prayer meetings seriously.

We have our prayer meetings on weekly basis. Every Wednesday between 5pm and 6pm. We believe so much in the power of prayers.
