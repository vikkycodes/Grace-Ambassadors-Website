Meet Our Pastors

Dr. Fadera Williams

About

Dr.Fadera Adebimpe Williams is a messenger of the Lord to the youth. She is a pioneer and the co-founding Pastor of Grace
Ambassadors World Outreach Ministry, a global youth ministry sent by God to raise a New Generation of Christ Centered
Leaders.
She received Christ as her Lord 29 years ago and she has been involoved in the ministry of our Lord Jesus Christ since then.

Professionally, she is a Landscape Architect, with a PhD in Architecture. She is the Founder and CEO of Hermon Empor Landscape Ltd,
an indigenous landscape architecture service firm as well as the President of Sustain Africa Earth Initiative, a non profit with a focus
on women, girls and the environment. She is a recipient of several awards and she is a fellow of several prestigious organisations such
as the US Department Vital Voices for Women, the prestigious Nigerian Leadership Initiative and the Rise Up For Women U.S.A to
mention but a few.
She is happily married to Pastor Akintoye Williams and a proud mother to two biological children.

Pastor Akintoye Williams

About

Pastor Akintoye Williams is the pioneer and co-founding Pastor of Grace Ambassadors Word Outreach Ministry, a global youth
ministry which is sent by God to raise a New Generation of Christ Centered Leaders.
Having served in different leadership capacities in ministry with over two decades experience, he has made it his life's mission to do the
will of God in reaching out to the world with specific interest in the youth.

Professionally, he is an Engineer and he is passionate about the construction industry where he runs a succesful business of
executing such projects. He is a great husband and a wonderful father to two lovely biological children. Happily married to Dr. Fadera
Williams.
