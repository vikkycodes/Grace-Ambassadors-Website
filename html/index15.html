Special Sundays

Our Sundays meetings are interesting and in fact, there are some days where we have interesting things happening.

On the third Sunday of the Month we have our Variety Sunday. It is a time where our Pastors sit and allow everyone to minister to them. Every activity in Church on that day is handled by the members, with exception to the Question and answer session where our Pastors helps to shed light on things that have not be clear throughout the Month.

Also we have our Love Feast Sunday, which is just a day where we all share and exchange gifts. It is just like a regular Sunday where we have our meetings, but we exchange gifts with one another just to foster love between ourselves, even as the Scriptures say in Hebrews13:1.
