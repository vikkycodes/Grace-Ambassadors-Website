Evangelism

We believe strongly that one of the reasons the Lord Jesus left the Church on earth is so that we can propagate his Gospel and populate heaven while depopulating hell.

It is therefore our responsibility to preach the gospel to all men as it is the plan of God for mankind to be saved and come to the knowledge of him.

For we are saved to save others, having the ministry of reconciliation entrusted into our hands.

As a ministry, we have set aside every Saturday as our official evangelism day. The time it takes place on Saturday is dependent on the geographical location of our partners. Regardless of your location, you can always join in and be a blessing to lives.

Every Saturday.
