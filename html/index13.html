Campus Outreaches

Our Campus outreaches are other platforms with which we reach out to students on our different campuses. As at today, we have several Campus Fellowships in the Southwestern and Southeastern parts of Nigeria and we trust God that we will continue to go on stronger. These Campus Outreaches are most times tagged Word and Power Conferences or Word and Worship Conferences, they are an awesome time to hear God's Word again.

As you will well know, our ministry is about heralding the Lordship of the Word, so we place premium on the teaching of God's Word.
